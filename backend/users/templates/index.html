UnTube

A simple Youtube playlist manager to help you modify and keep track of your YouTube playlists with ease.

Made with & by Mohammed Khan

UnTube automatically gets statistics for all your playlists when you first log in.

For all of your YouTube playlists, statistics like playlist duration, number of unavailable videos, or whether the playlist has any duplicate videos are automatically calculated.

Ideal for people with a lot of playlists on YouTube.

UnTube offers features like bulk deletion as well as copying and moving videos from one playlist to multiple other playlists at once.

Organize and search for playlists and videos in your collection.

UnTube organizes your playlists into specific categories. Further, you can create your own categories by tagging playlists. Since UnTube stores a reference to all the videos in your playlists, you can easily search for the videos you want.

Mark playlists as watching.

A small tool to keep track of the duration left for a playlist by letting you mark videos as watched. Might serve as a motivator to finish any kind of academic playlist

There's a public playlist you like? Import them into your collection.

You can import playlists you do not own into your UnTube collection. All the features above would still apply to playlists you do not own. That means you can mark imported playlists as watching, tag them and also copy videos from imported playlists into your own playlists.

And lastly, a beautiful dashboard.

Get greeted by some beautiful charts, view your most popular tags, and see a progress list of playlist you've marked as watching.

Some more features:

take notes on videos,

filter and sort playlists/videos,

auto-update your playlists with YouTube,

pin and like playlists/videos,

storing names of videos that go unavailable

About:

This app was made possible because of the YouTube Data API. By logging in with Google I get access to your public Google account information the permission to modify your YouTube data. That means you can manage your private playlists on UnTube.
